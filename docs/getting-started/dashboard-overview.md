# Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your home base on Autonomy Edge. Here's a quick tour of what you'll find and where everything leads.

---

## Navigation Bar

The navigation bar is at the top of every page. It has two key elements:

- **Autonomy Edge logo** (left): Click it from anywhere to return to the Dashboard. It's your "home" button.
- **Your avatar** (right): Click it to open a menu with links to your Profile, Documentation, What's New (changelog), Dark/Light mode toggle, and Logout.

> **Tip:** If there's a small dot on the "What's New" option, it means there are platform updates you haven't seen yet.

---

## Left Sidebar

The left sidebar gives you quick access to your projects and orchestrators.

### Projects

Shows your **5 most recent projects**. Click any project to open it directly in the IDE.

- Click the **New (+)** button to create a new project.
- Click **View all** at the bottom to see all your projects, organize folders, and manage settings.

### Orchestrators

Shows the orchestrators linked to your account. Click any orchestrator to see its details. Status, resource usage, and the devices attached to it.

- Click **Manage orchestrator** at the bottom to view all orchestrators or register a new one.

> **Tip:** If you're new and don't see any orchestrators, that's normal. You'll set one up in the [Quick Start Guide](./quick-start).

---

## Activity Feed

The center of the dashboard is the **Activity Feed**: a scrollable list of public projects from across the Autonomy Edge community.

Each project card shows the creator's name and avatar, a short project description, and how long ago it was updated. Click any card to open the project in the IDE, or click a user's name to visit their profile.

Use the **search bar** at the top to find projects or users by name.

> **Tip:** More projects load automatically as you scroll down.

---

## Right Sidebar

### Organizations

This section is a preview of an upcoming feature. Once organizations are available, your teams and groups will appear here.

### Latest Changes

Shows the most recent platform updates (changelog). You'll see a few highlights from the latest version. New features, fixes, and improvements. Click **View all** to see the full changelog.

---

## Floating Buttons

Two buttons float at the bottom-right corner of every page:

### Feedback

Click to share what you like, what's confusing, or what you'd like to see improved. Fill in at least one field and hit **Send feedback**.

### AI Chat Assistant ✨

Click the sparkle button (or press **⌘J** on Mac / **Ctrl+J** on Windows/Linux) to open the AI assistant. It can help you with:

- Platform features and navigation
- IEC 61131-3 programming concepts
- Setting up orchestrators and devices
- Writing and debugging your code

Press **Escape** or the same shortcut to close it.
