# User Profile

Your profile is how other users see you on Autonomy Edge. Here's how to access it, edit it, and customize your look.

---

## Accessing Your Profile

Click your **avatar** in the top-right corner of any page and select **Profile** from the dropdown menu.

You can also go directly to `edge.autonomylogic.com/profile`.

---

## Editing Your Profile

Click the **Edit Profile** button on your profile page. You can update:

- **Name**: Your display name, visible across the platform (required).
- **Username**: Your unique identifier, shown in project paths like `your-username / Project Name` (required, must be unique).
- **Bio**: A short description about yourself (optional). Use `@name` to mention organizations.
- **Location**: Your city, country, or region (optional).

> **Tip:** Your email is shown on this page but can't be changed here. It's used for authentication and is never visible to other users.

Click **Save** to apply your changes, or **Cancel** to discard them.

---

## Profile Photo

Your avatar appears throughout the platform. In the navigation bar, on project cards, and on your profile page. Click your avatar image to see your options:

### Upload a Photo

1. Click your avatar and select **Upload photo**.
2. Pick an image from your computer.
3. Drag to reposition and use the zoom slider to adjust.
4. Click **Use this photo**.

### Delete Your Photo

1. Click your avatar and select **Delete photo**.
2. Your photo is removed and replaced with your initials.

### No Photo?

If you haven't uploaded a photo, the platform shows your **initials** on a colored circle (e.g., "John Smith" → **JS**). You can customize both the letters and the color. See below.

---

## Customizing Your Initials

Prefer initials over a photo? You can pick custom letters and a background color.

1. Click your avatar and select **Customize initials**.
2. Enter up to **2 characters** for your initials.
3. Choose a **background color** from the palette.
4. Click **Save**.

Your custom initials are preserved even if you upload a photo later. They'll reappear if you ever remove the photo.

---

## Viewing Other Users' Profiles

You can view anyone's public profile by clicking their name or avatar anywhere on the platform (activity feed, project cards, etc.).

On another user's profile, you can see their name, username, bio, location, and public projects. You **can't** see their email address or edit anything.

---

## FAQ

**Can I change my email address?**
Not from the profile page. Contact support if you need to update your email.

**How do I change my password?**
Use the password recovery flow from the sign-in page. See the [Account Setup](./account-setup) guide for steps.

**Can I delete my account?**
Account deletion isn't available as a self-service feature yet. Contact Autonomy Logic support for assistance.
