# Settings → Profile

The Profile tab in your account settings is where your public-facing identity lives — name, username, bio, avatar, location, and time zone.

URL: `edge.autonomylogic.com/profile/settings?tab=profile`. Default tab when you open settings.

![Settings → Profile tab](images/profile-settings.png)

The page has a left side-nav listing every settings section:

- **Profile** *(current)*
- **Security** → Email, Password (sub-items expand)
- **Privacy**
- **Usage**
- **Billing**
- **Account**

A **Back to Dashboard** link at the very top returns you to your personal dashboard.

## Avatar section

- Your avatar circle and name on the right.
- *Click the avatar to upload or customize.* Instruction text below the row.

Clicking the avatar opens the upload dialog (PNG/JPG/GIF, square, up to 2 MB). See **[Editing your profile](../editing-your-profile)** for the upload requirements.

## Profile information

- **Name** — your display name. 80 characters max.
- **Username** — your unique handle. 3–30 characters, lowercase + digits + `-` + `_`.
- **Bio** — multi-line free text, up to 500 characters. Plain text.
- **Location** — free text.
- **Time zone** — dropdown of IANA time zones (*UTC*, *America/New_York*, *Europe/Berlin*, *Asia/Tokyo*, etc.).

A **Save changes** button at the bottom commits all of the above in one shot.

## What changing each field affects

| Field | Where the change shows up |
|---|---|
| Avatar | Header, profile, forum posts, commit cards, DM list, member lists. |
| Name | Profile, forum posts, commits, DMs. |
| Username | URLs, mentions, member lists, your forum identity. |
| Bio | Your profile. May appear in user hover-cards in the future. |
| Location | Your profile. |
| Time zone | Local-time display of timestamps in the editor and on some platform pages. |
