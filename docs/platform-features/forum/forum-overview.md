# Forum Overview

The forum is the public discussion space for the Autonomy Edge community. You don't need an account to **read** what's there, but you do need one to **post, reply, vote, or send direct messages**.

---

## Getting to the Forum

From anywhere in Autonomy Edge, click **Forum** in the top navigation. That takes you to the forum home page.

You can also open the forum directly at `edge.autonomylogic.com/forum`.

---

## What You See First

The forum home is organized into three horizontal bands:

### 1. Hero banner

A short headline and description at the top of the page. This is just branding. No buttons live here.

### 2. Toolbar

A row of counters and action buttons directly below the banner:

- **Total topics**: How many discussions have been created, ever.
- **Total posts**: How many replies have been written, across all topics.
- **Total members**: How many people have signed up.
- **Unread**: How many topics have new activity you haven't seen yet (logged-in users only). Only appears when you're logged in and the number is greater than zero.
- **Mark All Read**: Clears your unread indicator across every category at once. Only appears when you have unread content.
- **Members**: Jumps to the [Members Directory](members-directory).
- **New Topic**: Opens the [topic composer](creating-topics). If you're not logged in, you'll be asked to sign in first.

### 3. Main content area

This area switches between two views depending on what you're doing:

- **Categories view**: The default. Every category on the platform is shown as a card with its most recent activity.
- **Topics view**: Appears when you activate a sort (Latest, Trending, Most Replied, Most Viewed) or when you type something in the search box. Shows topics in a compact table.

We cover both views in detail in [Browsing Topics, Categories, and Search](browsing-categories).

---

## Forum vs. the Rest of the Platform

The forum is completely separate from your **projects**, **orchestrators**, and **vPLC devices**. Nothing you post on the forum affects your projects, and nothing in your projects is automatically shared to the forum.

The only pieces the forum shares with the rest of the platform are:

- **Your profile**: Name, username, avatar, and initials you set under Profile settings apply everywhere.
- **Your Privacy settings**: The toggles in your Privacy tab (see [Email and Privacy Preferences](email-preferences)) control which forum emails you receive.
- **Direct messages**: DMs and group chats live alongside the forum, but the messaging interface is its own section.

---

## Reading vs. Participating

| Action | Without an account | With an account |
|---|---|---|
| Read any topic | ✅ | ✅ |
| View categories, members, and profiles | ✅ | ✅ |
| Search topics | ✅ | ✅ |
| Create a topic | ❌ | ✅ |
| Reply | ❌ | ✅ |
| Upvote or downvote | ❌ | ✅ |
| Report a post | ❌ | ✅ |
| Receive email notifications | ❌ | ✅ |
| Send direct messages | ❌ | ✅ |

If you try to do something that requires an account (like clicking **Reply**), you'll see a prompt to sign in or create a free account.
