# Viewing and Replying to Topics

Click any topic title from the forum home or a category board to open the **thread detail** page. This is where the conversation lives. The original post, every reply, and the composer at the bottom for joining in.

---

## The Thread Header

At the top of every thread you'll see:

- **Back arrow** on the far left. Returns you to the category board you came from.
- **Topic title**: Big and bold. If you're the author, a pencil icon appears on hover to edit the title (see [Editing and Deleting Your Own Posts](editing-your-posts)).
- **Status badges**: Show up next to the title if relevant:
  - **Pinned** (gold): The topic is highlighted by the team; it stays at the top of its category.
  - **Locked** (amber): The topic is read-only; new replies are disabled.

On the right side of the header you'll find:

- **Share button**: Drops down a menu with:
  - **LinkedIn**, **X (Twitter)**, **Facebook**: Share the topic to that platform.
  - **Copy link**: Copies the topic URL to your clipboard. The button briefly says "Copied!" to confirm.
- **Reply button**: Scrolls the page to the reply composer at the bottom. If the topic is locked, this button is hidden.
- **Delete button**: Appears only if you created the topic (see [Editing and Deleting Your Own Posts](editing-your-posts)).

---

## The Posts

Every post. The original and every reply. Is shown as a card with the same structure:

### Who and when

- **Avatar** of the author on the left.
- **Author name**, clickable. Takes you to their profile.
- **Timestamp**: Relative time ("2h ago", "3d ago"). Hover for the exact date.
- **Edited label**: Small italic "· Edited" appears under the timestamp if the post has been edited.
- **Badge** next to the name:
  - **"Author"** (gold border): This user started the topic.
  - **"Member"** (gray border): Any other participant.

### The content

The body of the post, rendered with all its formatting: code blocks, lists, quotes, links, inline images, attachments. Clicking an inline image opens it full-size. Attachment links download when clicked.

### Action bar at the bottom

Three buttons below each post:

- **Upvote (👍)**: Marks the post as useful. Shows a count of everyone who upvoted.
  - Click once to upvote; click again to remove your upvote.
  - Highlighted in the brand color when your upvote is active.
- **Downvote (👎)**: The opposite. Marks the post as not useful. Also shows a count and toggles the same way.
- **Report (🚩)**: Opens the report dialog. Only visible on posts you didn't write. See [Reporting a Post](reporting-content).

> You need to be **signed in** to upvote, downvote, or report. Clicking these as an anonymous visitor takes you to the sign-in page.

### More menu (on your own posts)

If you wrote the post, a **•••** menu appears in the top-right corner of the post card with:

- **Edit**: Switches to the editor inline, with the same toolbar as the topic composer.
- **Delete**: Opens a confirmation dialog. The first post of a topic can't be deleted. You'd delete the entire topic from the header instead.

See [Editing and Deleting Your Own Posts](editing-your-posts) for full detail.

## Navigating Long Threads

Threads with more than **20 posts** get paginated. Navigation controls appear between groups of posts:

- **Previous** button. Goes to the previous page of posts.
- **Page indicator**: "Page X of Y (N total posts)".
- **Next** button. Goes to the next page.

Both buttons are disabled when you're already at the first or last page. Clicking them scrolls the page back to the top.

## Replying

The reply composer is anchored to the **bottom of every thread** (above the footer). It looks and behaves almost identically to the topic composer. Just without the category/title fields.

### If you're not signed in

Instead of the composer, you see a card that says:

> **Want to join the conversation?**
> Sign in to post a reply, or create a free account in seconds.

Two buttons:

- **Sign In**: Takes you to the sign-in page, and brings you back here when you're done.
- **Create Account**: Takes you to signup.

### If the topic is locked

You see a card that says:

> **This topic has been locked.** No new replies can be posted.

The composer isn't shown.

### If everything's fine

You see the reply composer. Key controls:

- **Write** and **Preview** tabs. Just like the topic composer.
- **Rich text toolbar**: Heading, bold, italic, underline, strikethrough, code block, link, bullet list, ordered list, quote.
- **@mention autocomplete**: Type `@` to open a small popup showing up to 8 people, starting with the topic author and recent posters. Keep typing to search by username. Click to insert.
- **Attachments and images**: Click the paperclip or image icons in the footer, or drag and drop into the editor. Max **10 MB per file**.
- **Reply button**: Posts the reply. While images are uploading, the button shows "Uploading images..." and is disabled.

Pressing Enter inside the editor adds a line break. It **does not** send. You have to click the Reply button.

## What Happens After You Reply

- Your reply is added to the bottom of the thread.
- The topic's activity timestamp updates, which bumps it in the Latest and Trending sorts.
- If the topic has an author (someone other than you), they get an in-app notification and. If they have it on. An email (see [Email and Privacy Preferences](email-preferences)).
- Any `@username` you typed also triggers an in-app notification and (if they opted in) an email to that user.
- The reply composer clears and resets so you can continue the conversation.

## Voting: What It Means

Votes aren't just cosmetic:

- **Upvotes** count toward the post author's **forum reputation**, which ranks them in the [Members Directory](members-directory) under "Most Reputed".
- **Downvotes** signal to the community (and to moderators) that a post is unhelpful or problematic.
- Only post authors see their own vote totals across all their posts (reflected in their reputation score).

You can only vote once per post. Clicking the same button twice removes your vote. Clicking the opposite button swaps from upvote to downvote (or vice versa).

## Quick Reference

| Action | Button | Requires sign-in? |
|---|---|---|
| Read a topic |. (just open the page) | No |
| Share on social | **Share** → LinkedIn / X / Facebook | No |
| Copy link | **Share** → Copy link | No |
| Upvote / downvote | 👍 / 👎 on a post | Yes |
| Report a post | 🚩 on a post | Yes |
| Reply | Reply button (header) or scroll to composer | Yes |
| Edit your post | **•••** on your post → Edit | Yes |
| Delete your post | **•••** on your post → Delete | Yes |
| Edit topic title | Pencil next to the title | Yes (own topic only) |
| Delete a topic | Delete button in header | Yes (own topic only) |
