# Browsing Topics, Categories, and Search

The forum home page has two ways of showing content: **by category** (the default) and **as a flat list of topics** (when you search or sort). This guide covers both, plus every filter and toggle you'll find.

---

## Categories View (Default)

When you first land on the forum home, you see a grid of **category cards**. This is the "at-a-glance" view. It shows you where the community is organized and which areas have new activity.

### What a category card shows

Each card contains:

- **Category name**: The big bold heading at the top.
- **Description**: A one-line summary, truncated if long.
- **Unread badge**: A bright badge with a number. Appears only if there are topics in this category you haven't seen. If the unread count is over 1,000, it shows as `1K+`.
- **Last topic preview**: The title of the most recent topic and when it was posted ("2 days ago"). Click the title to jump straight to that topic.
- **Stats row**: `N topics` and `N posts` for the whole category.
- **Sub-category pills**: Small clickable badges for each sub-section of this category. Each pill may have its own unread badge.

### What happens when you click

- Click the **category name** or anywhere on the card → opens the category board, which lists every topic in it.
- Click a **sub-category pill** → opens the board for that sub-category specifically.
- Click the **last topic preview** → jumps directly to that topic.

## Topics View (When You Search or Sort)

As soon as you **type in the search box** or click one of the **sort tabs** (Latest / Trending / Most Replied / Most Viewed), the view switches. Instead of category cards, you see topics listed as rows in a table.

### The sort tabs

Four mutually-exclusive buttons above the content area:

| Tab | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Latest** | Most recently updated topics first. Good for catching up on what's happening right now. |
| **Trending** | Topics with the most activity relative to how new they are. What's heating up. |
| **Most Replied** | Topics ranked by total reply count. The longest conversations. |
| **Most Viewed** | Topics ranked by how many people opened them. The most-seen content. |

Click a tab to activate it. Click it **again** to deactivate and go back to categories view.

### The search box

- Placeholder: "Search topics..."
- Type anything and the list filters automatically after a short pause (roughly half a second).
- The results header changes to `Results for "your query" (N)` so you know how many matched.
- Clear the search box to return to whatever sort was active, or to categories view.

### Topic row columns

Each row in the topics table shows:

- **Pin icon** (gold): If a topic is **pinned**, a small pin appears on the far left. Pinned topics are marked by the team as important; they stay at the top of their category regardless of activity.
- **Lock icon** (gray): If a topic is **locked**, a lock appears on the left. Locked topics can still be read but can't be replied to.
- **Topic title**: Clickable. Hover highlights it. Long titles truncate with `…`.
- **Category**: The category or sub-category name, shown smaller under the title.
- **Users**: Avatars of the topic author and the most recent replier, stacked next to each other.
- **Replies**: The total reply count.
- **Likes**: The total upvote count across all posts in the topic. Shown with a heart icon. Hidden when zero.
- **Views**: How many people have opened the topic. `1K+` format above 1,000.
- **Activity**: A relative timestamp for the most recent post ("2h ago", "3d ago").

Click the topic title or row to open the thread.

### Pagination

Long lists are split into pages of **25 topics** each. At the bottom you'll see:

- **Previous** button. Goes to the previous page (disabled on page 1).
- Page indicator. "Page X of Y (N total)".
- **Next** button. Goes to the next page (disabled on the last page).

## Category Board (When You Click a Category)

Clicking a category opens a page dedicated to that area. The layout looks similar to the topics view but is automatically filtered to topics in that category (and all its sub-categories, if the category has any).

You can still use the same sort tabs and search box on a category board. They work within the category only.

---

## Sub-Categories

Some categories have sub-categories for finer organization. On the home page, sub-category pills appear under each category card. Clicking a pill opens that sub-category's board (equivalent to: "show me only topics filed under this more-specific heading").

Topics are always filed under **either** a category **or** a sub-category, never both at the same time.

---

## Quick Reference

| Element | Where it is | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| Search box | Top of content area | Filter topics by keyword (type anywhere on the home page) |
| Sort tabs | Top of content area | Switch to the flat topic list, sorted differently |
| Category card | Default home view | Click to open that category's board |
| Sub-category pill | On a category card | Open a more specific section |
| Pin icon | Left of a topic title | Marks topics highlighted by the team |
| Lock icon | Left of a topic title | Topic is read-only. No new replies allowed |
| Unread badge | On category cards and the toolbar | Count of new topics you haven't viewed |
| Mark All Read | Toolbar (when unread > 0) | Clears your unread indicator everywhere |
| Pagination | Bottom of the topics table | Navigate through long lists, 25 per page |
