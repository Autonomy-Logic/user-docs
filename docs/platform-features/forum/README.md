# Community Forum

The Autonomy Edge forum is where the community asks questions, shares projects, troubleshoots hardware, and learns from each other. This section walks you through every part of the forum from an end-user perspective. How to find topics, start discussions, reply with code and images, message people directly, and control which emails you get.

---

## What You Can Do

- **Browse and search**: Explore topics by category, filter the list by trending or most-replied, or search across every discussion.
- **Start a topic**: Ask a question or share something you built, with a full rich-text editor (code blocks, lists, images, attachments).
- **Reply and vote**: Upvote helpful posts, downvote unhelpful ones, and reply using the same editor. Tag teammates with `@username`.
- **Edit and delete your own posts**: Fix typos or remove a reply you no longer want.
- **Direct messages**: Chat one-on-one with another member or create a group conversation.
- **Report problems**: Flag posts that are spam, off-topic, or inappropriate.
- **Control your notifications**: Fine-grained email toggles in your Privacy settings (weekly digest, replies, @mentions, unread-message reminders, group invites).
