# Members Directory

The Members page is a leaderboard for the community. Who posts the most, who has the best reputation, who just joined, and a full alphabetical index of everyone. Open it from the **Members** button on the forum home toolbar or the breadcrumb inside any category.

**Direct URL:** `edge.autonomylogic.com/forum/members`

---

## Page Layout

At the top you see:

- **Breadcrumb**: `Forum > Members`.
- **Page title**: "Members".
- **Member count**: Top-right: "N members".

Below that is a row of four tabs that switch what's displayed.

---

## The Four Tabs

### 🏆 Top Posters

Members ranked by **total number of posts** they've written. Someone who asks a lot of questions and answers a lot of replies climbs this list.

- **Period selector** (dropdown, top-right of the content area):
  - **This Month**: Counts only posts from the current month. The default.
  - **All Time**: Counts every post ever, since the user signed up.
- **Podium (page 1 only)**: If there are at least 3 members with posts in the selected period, the top 3 are shown in a dedicated podium row:
  - **#1**: Gold crown, large avatar, post count.
  - **#2**: Silver medal, avatar, post count.
  - **#3**: Bronze award, avatar, post count.
- Below the podium, the rest of the list appears as a grid of member cards.

### ⭐ Most Reputed

Members ranked by **forum reputation**. Reputation is the sum of upvotes on everything they've written, minus downvotes. Writing content the community finds helpful moves you up this list.

- Same **This Month / All Time** period selector as Top Posters.
- Same podium treatment for #1 / #2 / #3.
- Each card shows a **🔥 flame icon** next to members with reputation greater than 1.

### 👤 New Members

People who **joined recently**, ordered by signup date (newest first). No period selector on this tab. It's always "show me the most recent people".

Each card shows "Joined [Month Year]" instead of a count.

### 🔎 All Members

The **complete membership list**, alphabetical by name. This tab has two extra controls:

- **Search box**: Placeholder "Search by name...". Filters the list as you type.
- **Alphabet strip**: A row of buttons: `# A B C D ... Z`. Click a letter to filter to members whose name starts with it. Click the same letter again to clear. The `#` bucket holds names that start with a number or symbol. Searching clears any active letter filter.

Each card on this tab shows both **post count** and **reputation**.

---

## Member Cards

Every tab shows members as cards in a grid (1 column on phones, 2 on tablets, 3–4 on desktop).

Every card has:

- **Avatar**: Their photo, or initials on a colored circle if they haven't uploaded one.
- **Name**: The display name they chose.
- **Stats**: Varies by tab (see above).
- **Rank number**: On Top Posters and Most Reputed, a rank is shown in the bottom-right of the card for positions 4 and below.

Clicking anywhere on the card takes you to that person's **profile page**, where you can read their bio, see what they've posted, and start a direct message (see [Direct Messages and Group Chats](direct-messages)).

---

## Pagination

All four tabs use the same pagination:

- **24 members per page**.
- **Previous** / **Next** buttons at the bottom of the grid.
- Page indicator: "Page X of Y (N total)".

---

## Empty States

- **No members match your search**: Shows a centered "No members found" message.
- **No letter match**: Same message.

---

## How Reputation Is Calculated

Reputation is a single number that reflects how useful your posts have been to the community. The rough formula:

- **+1** for each upvote you receive on any of your posts.
- **−1** for each downvote you receive.

The number you see on your profile and on the Most Reputed leaderboard is the net total.

> **Tip:** Reputation takes time to build. If you want to climb the Most Reputed list, focus on **quality answers**: well-formatted, specific, with code examples and screenshots. A single great answer often earns more upvotes than a dozen one-liners.
